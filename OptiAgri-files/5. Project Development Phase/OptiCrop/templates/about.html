About OptiCrop

OptiCrop turns soil and environmental readings into crop recommendations, confidence scores, and basic advisory information.

Pipeline

How It Works

The training pipeline cleans the Crop Recommendation Dataset, generates EDA visualizations, trains five classical machine learning models, compares their accuracy, and saves the best performer with Joblib.

The Flask application validates user inputs, loads the saved model, predicts the crop, estimates confidence, scores soil health, and returns practical crop guidance.

1Collect

N, P, K, temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall.

2Train

Decision Tree, Random Forest, SVM, Logistic Regression, and KNN.

3Recommend

Return crop, confidence, soil status, season, water, and fertilizer guidance.
